About

Home About


Hello I’m Gerard Tiernan your local heating and plumbing engineer.

With over 20 years experience and hands-on knowledge of installations, repairs and maintenance I’m confident I can help you protect your heating systems to give you greater efficiency and savings.

As a family man living in Cleeve I know the importance of having hot and cold water literally ‘on-tap’ in order to run an orderly and efficient home!

I first staring working in this trade in 1997 and to this day I still get great satisfaction from transforming a previously cold house or ineffective system into a system that works like a dream.

During my time as a plumbing and heating engineer I have gained specialists knowledge in renewable energy systems and both solid fuel and oil systems. And for the last 7 years I have also been passing on my knowledge of this industry to students in Further Education as a Plumbing Lecturer.

When I’m not fixing pipework I enjoy spending time with my wife and our two young boys and when I need something less energetic to do you’ll find me on the golf-course or playing tennis for our local club!

I pride myself on quality of work, reliability and a no-nonsense service.

Have a particular issue? Then please do give me a call on 07832 9688706 or complete our simple enquiry form – I’d be only too delighted to help.
